Screenshots

Select a Lab (the main app page).
Creating a lab is quick and easy.
Managing your labs is easy, and adding students is as simple as sending them a code.
Professors and teaching assistants can choose to receive notifications via email. Professors can also remove users from the lab.
Professors and teaching assistants can easily accept students' request for help at the click of a button.
Professors and teaching assistants can easily mark a request as complete or transfer the request to another TA or professor.
Professors can monitor students' requests for help as they learn in labs. Professors can also monitor teaching assistants' feedback. They can optionally allow the TAs to see this information.
Professors can easily manage their subscription options and upgrade if needed. TA and student accounts are completely free.
Students and teaching assistants can easily select a lab and see if there are pending requests at a glance.
Students and teaching assistants can easily add labs to their accounts using the lab codes professors provide.
Students can easily submit requests for help and see how many people are ahead of them in line.
After the request is marked complete, students can quickly leave feedback and get back to learning.
Students can easily see the requests they've submitted in their labs in the past.
