Q: How many students and TAs can I have in my lab?
A: You can have as many as you like!

Q: What if I have multiple sections for a lab?
A: You should create a separate lab for each section and label them with the appropriate section number

Q: How do I remove a student or TA from my lab?
A: Head over to lab settings to remove users from the lab. If a user still has a lab code and attempts to add themselves back, please regenerate a new lab code
